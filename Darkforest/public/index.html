Controls

WASD to move, Left-click to fire, Spacebar for flashlight.

Game

Currently, a 1v1 sandbox in which each player has one life and loses upon hit.

Each player is represented by a colored circle, blue or red, and also has their own vision circle.

Using your flashlight allows you to see farther in a direction, but also exposes your own location.

Weapons

Default: 2.5 second reload, one shot.
